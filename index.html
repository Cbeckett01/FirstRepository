The Princess Bride is an action/comedy movie about the love between Westly and Buttercup. At the beginning of the movie, Buttercup believes Westly to be dead, only to realize that he is alive, and then lose him again. Westly is saved and recieves help from Fezzik and Inigo and saves her. Then they kiss, and the story is over.
